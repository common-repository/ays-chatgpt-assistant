### AI Assistant with ChatGPT by AYS

AI Assistant with ChatGPT plugin can create content for your blog, answer any questions you would like, or even write HTML, CSS, or any other programming codes for you right from your WordPress dashboard. The most popular AI chatbot is already available for WordPress users. You will no longer be alone as your assistant is there for you whenever you need. 

After activating the plugin, the AI Assistant with ChatGPT window will pop up at the **bottom-right corner of your WordPress dashboard**. This advanced chatbot can answer your follow-up questions like a human-like conversation.

== HOW TO USE ==

1. Sign up [here](https://beta.openai.com/signup) or log in if you already have an account in the OpenAI platform. You can use your Google or Microsoft account to sign up if you don`t want to create an account by entering an email/password combination. You may need a valid mobile number to verify your account. 
2. Then, you need to visit your [OpenAI key page](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys). 
3. Create a new key by clicking the "+ Create new secret key" button.
4. Copy the key, go back to your WordPress dashboard, and paste it into the provided box.
5. Click on the "Connect" button.

You're done!
